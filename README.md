FireworkFix makes Firework Rockets calculate their damage linearly based on the number of Firework Stars.

Every part of the mod is configurable.

**Damage Table:**

| Firework Stars | Vanilla Damage | FireworkFix Damage |
| :------------: | :------------: | :----------------: |
| 0              | 0              | 0                  |
| 1              | 6              | 4                  |
| 2              | 8              | 8                  |
| 3              | 10             | 12                 |
| 4              | 12             | 16                 |
| 5              | 14             | 20                 |
| 6              | 16             | 24                 |
| 7              | 17             | 28                 |
